---
title: "Lifetime"
source_path: "c/language/lifetime"
category: "c"
---

Every [object](/c/language/object/) in C exists, has a constant address, retains its last-stored value (except when the value is indeterminate), and, for VLA, retains its size(since C99) over a portion of program execution known as this object's lifetime.
