---
title: "Elaborated type specifier"
source_path: "cpp/language/elaborated_type_specifier"
category: "language"
---

Elaborated type specifiers may be used to refer to a previously-declared class name (class, struct, or union) or to a previously-declared enum name even if the name was [hidden by a non-type declaration](/cpp/language/lookup/). They may also be used to declare new class names.
