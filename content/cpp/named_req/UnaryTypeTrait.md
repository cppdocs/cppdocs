title: "C++ named requirements: UnaryTypeTrait (since C++11)"
source_path: "cpp/named_req/UnaryTypeTrait"
category: "named_req"
since: "C++11"
---

A UnaryTypeTrait is a class template that describes a property of its template type parameter with an [std::integral_constant](/cpp/types/integral_constant/) (typically [std::bool_constant](/cpp/types/integral_constant/)) specialization.
