title: "C++ named requirements: BinaryTypeTrait (since C++11)"

category: "named_req"
since: "C++11"
---

A BinaryTypeTrait is a class template that describes a relationship between its two template type parameters with a [std::integral_constant](/cpp/types/integral_constant/) (typically [std::bool_constant](/cpp/types/integral_constant/)) specialization.
