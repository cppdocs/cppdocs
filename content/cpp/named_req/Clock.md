The Clock requirements describe a bundle consisting of a [std::chrono::duration](/cpp/chrono/duration/), a [std::chrono::time_point](/cpp/chrono/time_point/), and a function now() to get the current time_point. The origin of the clock's time_point is referred to as the clock's epoch.
