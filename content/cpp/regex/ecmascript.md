This page describes the regular expression grammar that is used when [std::basic_regex](/cpp/regex/basic_regex/) is constructed with [syntax_option_type](/cpp/regex/syntax_option_type/) set to ECMAScript (the default). See [syntax_option_type](/cpp/regex/syntax_option_type/) for the other supported regular expression grammars.
