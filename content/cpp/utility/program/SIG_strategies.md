The SIG_DFL and SIG_IGN macros expand into integral expressions that are not equal to an address of any function. The macros define signal handling strategies for [std::signal](/cpp/utility/program/signal/)() function.

## Declarations
```cpp
#define SIG_DFL /*implementation defined*/
```

```cpp
#define SIG_IGN /*implementation defined*/
```
