title: "C++ keyword: typename"
source_path: "cpp/keywords/typename"

In the template parameter list of a [template declaration](/cpp/language/templates/), typename can be used as an alternative to [class](/cpp/keyword/class/) to declare [type template parameters](/cpp/language/template_parameters/#Type_template_parameter)and template template parameters(since C++17).
